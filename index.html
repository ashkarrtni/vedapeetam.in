About Us

Spirituality and materiality are movements in two directions from a single point, the mind. In this, I refer to the movement towards the self as spirituality and the movement towards the material as materiality. But what can be understood from this material world is that humanity has not yet understood the truth that both of these originate from the mind. In this material world, humanity exists because of interdependence. In a sense, it can exist. Although all living beings except humans depend on each other to some extent, living beings do not depend on each other for the needs of life. Instead, they depend on nature. But humans exist through interdependence rather than dependence on nature. The result of all good and bad events that occur in the interdependent human race affects only the human mind. Man is the owner of a mind that cannot recognize or understand this truth. What is the greatest power in this material world? Have you ever thought.! The greatest power, is the human mind, which works in the form of mysterious energy.
A man is exhausted by his mind, but that means that the greatest power in the world has been broken. Humans have found many ways to recover from such a breakdown. This is called entertainment. Writing, reading, acting, singing, various games, etc. are all exercises for the human mind. The benefits of exercising in any art are greater than the benefits it gives to the body or the pleasure it gives to the mind. All this is possible because of interdependence. I intended my writing style to be like this. My intention was that the essence of my spirituality should be useful to those who are pursuing education in any field, those who are acquiring similar knowledge, and those who are doing research. Combining spirituality, modernity and materialism, I am treading a path that the scientific world has not yet traveled, and readers can join me on my journey, whether through writing or through a spiritual journey. I understand that one can only progress in life through one's innate ability when one is aligned with one's interest, spiritual essence or aptitude. The aim of Veda Peetam Spiritual Research Foundation is to enable readers to acquire material knowledge by spiritually awakening the doubts, questions and wonders inherent within them
